Masaai Mara

Touring Maasai Mara is an unforgettable experience, offering a front-row seat to one of Africa’s most iconic wildlife spectacles. Maasai Mara National Reserve is a world-renowned wildlife conservation area in southwestern Kenya, named after the Maasai people and the Mara River that runs through it. It's an extension of Tanzania’s Serengeti National Park, forming a massive ecosystem rich in biodiversity.

Amboseli

Amboseli National Park — one of Kenya’s most scenic and iconic safari destinations. is located in southern Kenya near the Tanzania border, with the majestic Mount Kilimanjaro as its backdrop. The park is famous for its large elephant herds, stunning landscapes, and sweeping views of Africa’s tallest mountain. Known as the best place in Africa to get up-close views of huge herds of elephants, especially with Kilimanjaro looming in the background.
